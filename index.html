About

“He draws inspiration from the authentic beauty of fleeting moments — a sunset just before it turns, a fabric caught mid-motion.”

Cedou is a photographer and creative director working across editorial and commercial visual content, shaped by the particular light of the French Riviera he grew up around.

His work moves between travel, design, lifestyle and fashion, always in search of the detail that makes a moment worth keeping. He collaborates directly with brands and artists, treating every image as part of a larger story rather than a single frame.

He shoots under his own name, and sometimes through Studio Ciel Rose.
